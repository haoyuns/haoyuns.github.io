about

Hi there, I'm Haoyun, a storyteller with a focus on visualization for presenting data to everyone.

My favorite programming language is R, but I also design with QGIS, Adobe Illustrator and Sketch.
I've worked as a lecturer at Communication University of China, as a consultant at the Centre for Humanitarian Data, and as a graphics editor or data journalist in mainstream newsrooms in both China and the U.S.

Workshops and webinars:

  • April 28, 2020: Online webinar about Covid-19 data for Communication University of China
  • July 11-15, 2019 @ Beijing: Data visualization workshop for Renmin University
  • Jan. 10-12, 2018 @ Kunming, Yunnan: Data storytelling workshop with Wilson Center and 20 NGOs
  • July 28-31, 2016 @ Beijing: GIS mapping workshop with NICAR (University of Missouri-Columbia)
  • April 26, 2015: Online webinar about open data for Wuhan University
